GHANA VADYA / IDIOPHONES

Avanaddha vadya are percussion instruments. Such instruments are made by Stretching the animal skin across a metal or earthen pot, wooden barrel or frame. The sound is produced by striking the animal skin that has been stretched across the body. The earliest reference to such instruments could be found in the Vedas.
